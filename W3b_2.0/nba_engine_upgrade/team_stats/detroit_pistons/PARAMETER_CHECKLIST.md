# Detroit Pistons — Renaissance Parameter Checklist
### *Every data point needed to generate a prediction for this team*

---

## 1. Core Performance Metrics
- [ ] Elo Rating (current + 30-day trend)
- [ ] Net Rating (offensive rating minus defensive rating, per 100 possessions)
- [ ] SRS (Simple Rating System — schedule-adjusted margin)
- [ ] Record (overall, home, away, last 10, last 20)
- [ ] ATS Record (against the spread)
- [ ] Over/Under record (high or low scoring tendency)
- [ ] Point differential (average margin of victory/defeat)
- [ ] Pythagorean win % (expected win rate based on points scored vs allowed)

## 2. Pace & Style Profile
- [ ] Pace (possessions per 48 minutes)
- [ ] Offensive style breakdown (% isolation, pick-and-roll, transition, post-up, spot-up, cut)
- [ ] Three-point attempt rate (3PA / FGA)
- [ ] Free throw rate (FTA / FGA)
- [ ] Turnover rate (turnovers per 100 possessions)
- [ ] Rebounding rate (offensive + defensive rebound %)
- [ ] Assist rate (% of made baskets that are assisted)

## 3. Injury & Roster State
- [ ] Current injuries (player name, status, expected return)
- [ ] Elo impact per injured player
- [ ] BPM sum of available roster (Box Plus/Minus of who's playing)
- [ ] Minutes distribution (star minutes vs load management)
- [ ] Depth rating (performance drop-off when bench is playing)

## 4. Schedule & Fatigue
- [ ] Rest days (days since last game)
- [ ] Back-to-back status (1st of B2B, 2nd of B2B, or rested)
- [ ] Games in last 7 days (fatigue accumulation)
- [ ] Travel miles in last 7 days
- [ ] Time zone crossings (circadian disruption)
- [ ] Altitude factor (Denver/Utah visitor penalty)
- [ ] Schedule spot (trap game, look-ahead, letdown, rivalry)

## 5. Regime & Momentum (HMM)

- [ ] Performance trend (last 5 net rating vs season average)

## 6. Matchup-Specific

## 7. Market & Betting Profile

## 8. Coaching & System

- [ ] Coach ATS record
- [ ] Timeout usage patterns

## 9. Referee Sensitivity
- [ ] Foul rate by ref crew
- [ ] Home vs away whistle differential by ref

- [ ] Free throw dependence (FT-reliant teams are more ref-sensitive)

## 10. Advanced / Renaissance-Specific
