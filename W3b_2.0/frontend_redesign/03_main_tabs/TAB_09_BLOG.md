# Main Tab 9: Blog (Market Research & Thesis)

## The Objective
Quantitative funds don't write "blogs," they publish "Research Papers" and "Market Commentary". This tab establishes authority by deeply analyzing macro trends in the sports/prediction market ecosystems.

## Language & Nomenclature Shift
- **❌ BANNED RETAIL TERMS:** Updates, News, My Thoughts.
- **✅ REQUIRED INSTITUTIONAL TERMS:** Market Thesis, Research Papers, Regime Analysis, Quantitative Commentary, Whitepapers.

## Visual Implementation Plan
1. **The Academic Layout:**
   - Extremely clean, readable typography (Geist or Inter). High-contrast reading experience that resembles a scientific paper or a Substack written by a math PhD.
2. **Data-Rich Thumbnails:**
   - Instead of stock photos of basketball players, the article thumbnails are actually just stark, high-contrast charts, graphs, or code snippets from the engine that represent the core thesis of the article.
3. **Filter by Alpha:**
   - Articles are categorized not by "date" but by "Research", "System Updates", and "Market Inefficiency Discoveries".
