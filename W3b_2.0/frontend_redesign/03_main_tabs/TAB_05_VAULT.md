# Main Tab 5: Vault (The Capital Reserve)

## The Objective
This tab visually demonstrates the real-time financial trajectory ("the rise") of the fund. It proves the system is highly lucrative. Instead of just a "balance" screen, this represents the **Vault**: a highly secure, growing pool of capital.

## Language & Nomenclature Shift
- **❌ BANNED RETAIL TERMS:** Wallet, My Money, Withdrawals, Winnings.
- **✅ REQUIRED INSTITUTIONAL TERMS:** Treasury, Liquidity Pool, Capital Accumulation, Locked Value, Yield Generation Rate.

## Visual Implementation Plan
1. **The Treasury Waterfall:**
   - A stunning 3D visual—potentially a slowly rising liquid gold simulation or a massive glowing numerical counter—representing the ever-increasing Total Value Locked (TVL) of the fund.
2. **Real-Time Yield Ticker:**
   - A highly technical readout showing the exact rate of capital accumulation (e.g., "$X.XX per hour" or "+Y% daily compounding").
3. **The "Rise" Chart:**
   - A stark, aggressive, and perfectly clear logarithmic or linear chart proving the upward trajectory of the capital base since inception, completely decoupled from individual trades and focused purely on aggregate fund growth.
